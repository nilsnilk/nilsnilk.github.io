ABOUT ME

My name is Nils Sundberg and I am 27 years old. I study at Umeå University in Sweden, and I am currently finished with the third year out of a five year civil engineering programme called "interaktion och design". The name of this programme roughly translates from swedish into "Interaction design", and mainly concerns the subjects of computer science and interaction design respectively. The interdiciplinary nature of the programme (also the interaction design subject in itself) makes the courses very versatile, which gives the students the freedom to niche themselves in some degree. I have found myself to enjoy programming in general, and making small conceptual programmes such as mobile and web applications in particular.
